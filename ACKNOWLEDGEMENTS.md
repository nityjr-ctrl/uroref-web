## Clinical Content

Deep Dives and on-call summaries on this site are written by Nity G (Urology SpR) and are referenced to, adapted from, or paraphrased from published guidelines and literature. Nothing is reproduced verbatim.

- **European Association of Urology (EAU) Guidelines** — 2024 edition. <https://uroweb.org/guidelines>
- **NICE** — NG12 (Suspected cancer referral), NG131 (Prostate cancer), NG203 (Renal and ureteric stones) and related guidance.
- **British Association of Urological Surgeons (BAUS)** — consent guidance and patient-information leaflets. <https://www.baus.org.uk>
- **AJCC/UICC** — Cancer Staging Manual, 8th Edition.
- **British National Formulary (BNF)** — for drug dosing.
- Peer-reviewed journal articles cited inline in individual Deep Dives.
