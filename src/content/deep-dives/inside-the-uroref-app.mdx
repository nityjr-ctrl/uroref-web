The emergency module is the heart of the app. Each tree is built around the question you're actually asking at 2am — not a textbook walkthrough, but a binary decision path that gets you to the right action quickly.

## Drug Doses

A curated reference for the drugs that actually come up in urology on call. NSAIDs, alpha-blockers, antibiotics for urosepsis, intravesical agents, hormone therapy doses — with the specific context (indication, dose, route, contraindications) you need in the moment.

> No scrolling through BNF pages at 3am. The drugs are listed by clinical scenario, not alphabetically.

## Clinic Frameworks

Structured frameworks for outpatient urology — covering the common clinic presentations from new haematuria to lower urinary tract symptoms to post-operative follow-up. These are particularly useful early in training when the structure of a urology consultation is still unfamiliar.

## 31 BAUS Patient Leaflets

The full suite of BAUS patient information leaflets, in-app and available offline. Useful in clinic when a patient needs to take something away, or when you need to quickly check what patients are routinely told about a procedure before a consent conversation.
